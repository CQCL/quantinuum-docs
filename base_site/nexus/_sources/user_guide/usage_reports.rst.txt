Usage Reports
=============

Usage reports can be generated to show your individual usage of Quantinuum Systems.

You can view your usage reports by navigating to the "Organization" tab on the settings page, under the "My Usage Reports" section.

Create Usage Reports
------------------------

You can generate a usage report in the "Organization" tab on the settings page, under the "My Usage Reports" section.

From here you can click "Create Report" which will open a dialog where you can name your report, set the expiration length and select the date range for the report.

.. note::
   Once a report has expired it will no longer be available for download.
