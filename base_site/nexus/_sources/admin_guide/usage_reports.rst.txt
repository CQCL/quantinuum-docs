Usage Reports
=============

Usage reports can be generated to show your organization's usage of Quantinuum hardware and emulators.

You can view usage reports for your organization by navigating to the "Quantinuum Systems Reports" tab on your organizations admin page.

Users are able to generate individual reports on their usage. You can find out more about this in the `User Guide <../user_guide/usage_reports>`_.

Creating Usage Reports
------------------------

You can generate a usage report for your organization by navigating to the "Quantinuum Systems" reports tab on your organizations admin page.

From here you can click "Create Report" which will open a dialog where you can name your report, set the expiration length and select the date range for the report.

.. note::
   Once a report has expired it will no longer be available for download.
