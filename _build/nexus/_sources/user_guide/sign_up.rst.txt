Signing Up
==========

Quantinuum Nexus is currently available as a beta product in a preview window. During the preview window the product is not generally available, but you can request access by going 
to https://nexus.quantinuum.com/signup and filling out the form there.

Unfortunately, not everyone will be able to access Nexus during this preview period, but everyone who registers their interest will be kept informed as the product moves towards release.

The preview window is expected to last for 2 months and for the duration Quantinuum Nexus is provided free of charge.
