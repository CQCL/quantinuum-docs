Premium Features
================

Premium features gives your organization access to additional features in Nexus.
Features include access to premium devices, software and tools.

Once a feature is enabled for your organization, you will be able to see your access to the feature in the "Software" section in the "Access" tab on your organization's admin page.
Features enabled for your organization will be available to all users in your organization.

How do I access a premium feature?
-----------------------------------
Premium features are enabled by your organization's Nexus administrators.
You can contact us to upgrade your access to additional features.
Send an email to Nexus support at nexus_support@quantinuum.com, to inquire about upgrading your organization's access.
