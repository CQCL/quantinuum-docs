How to Cite Quantinuum Nexus
=============================

This document provides information on how to cite use of the Quantinuum Nexus platform for 
third-party academic papers. If you would like to reference the Nexus platform in another context,
such as a press release, please contact Quantinuum at `press@quantinuum.com <mailto:press@quantinuum.com>`_.



Research papers
-----------------

All research papers that use Quantinuum Nexus as part of their work should cite the platform.


.. code-block:: text

    Quantinuum Nexus. https://nexus.quantinuum.com/, 2024.

Example Bibtex entry:

.. code-block:: bibtex

    @misc{quantinuum_nexus,
      title={Quantinuum Nexus},
      url={https://nexus.quantinuum.com/},
      year={2024},
    }
